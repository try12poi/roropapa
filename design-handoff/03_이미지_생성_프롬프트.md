## 재생성 요청 문구 (결과가 어긋났을 때)

- 등신이 길게 나옴 → `Make it much more chibi: head must be almost as big as the body. 2.5 heads tall total.`
- 무섭게 나옴 → `Too scary. Make it rounder, sillier, and more adorable. A child should find it cute.`
- 적이 컬러풀함 → `The monster must be monochrome dark navy-gray with ONLY red glowing eyes as color.`
- 배경이 붙어 나옴 → `Plain white background only. No environment.`
- 일본풍 편의점 → `This must be a KOREAN convenience store: Korean signage, banana milk, samgak-gimbap, parasol table.`
